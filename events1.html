We're coming to the Vancouver Fan Expo!

Vancouver Fan Expo is a convention that highlights celebrities, shopping, panels and workshops, It is the place to be to find the newest and hottest in Anime, Manga, Comics, Sci-Fi, Gaming and Horror.
